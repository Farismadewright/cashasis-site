How quickly can you close?+
We can close in as little as 7 days. If you need more time, that's fine too. You choose the closing date that works for you.
Do I need to clean or make repairs before selling?+
No. Sell completely as-is. Leave behind whatever you don't want, skip the cleanup, and never lift a hammer. We handle it from there.
Is this a real offer or just an estimate?+
It's a real, no-obligation cash offer based on your actual property and situation, not a vague online estimate.
Are there any fees or commissions?+
None. No agent commissions, no listing fees, and no hidden costs. The offer we present is what you walk away with.
What if I'm behind on payments or in foreclosure?+
We can still help. We've worked with sellers behind on payments and facing foreclosure, and we move quickly to beat tight deadlines.
What areas do you buy in?+
We buy throughout the local area and surrounding communities. Not sure if we cover yours? Reach out and we'll let you know.
